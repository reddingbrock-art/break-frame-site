About Break Frame Construction

A Fort Worth framing company built on precision, reliability, and honest work.

Meet the Owner

Joel Rodriguez

Joel Rodriguez founded Break Frame Construction with one goal: deliver framing and exterior work that holds up — structurally and under scrutiny. With over 10 years of hands-on experience in the construction industry, Joel has built a reputation for precision craftsmanship and dependable execution.

Based in Fort Worth and serving the broader DFW area, Break Frame Construction works with homebuilders, general contractors, and property managers who need a framing crew they can count on to show up, stay on schedule, and do the work right.

From new construction framing to specialty trim and exterior siding, every project is treated with the same level of care — whether it's a single-family home or a large commercial build.
